BE THE PART OF OUR FAMILY

Guiding the Young Generation to Success.

Divine Public School is a leading educational institution committed to nurturing holistic, future-ready learners. Rooted in the principles of the National Curriculum Framework (NCF) 2023 and the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, our school offers a competency-based, inclusive, and technology-integrated learning environment. We focus not only on academic excellence but also on the overall development of every child—fostering creativity, critical thinking, empathy, and leadership. Through active parent-teacher partnerships and a student-centric approach, we strive to create a vibrant, supportive ecosystem where every child thrives.

Our Mission

  • Seamlessly implement NCF 2023 and NEP 2020 for a competency-driven curriculum.
  • Promote experiential, inquiry-based, and interdisciplinary learning.
  • Develop multilingual proficiency, integrating regional languages and English.
  • Foster innovation with AI, VR, and STEM education.
  • Encourage ethical values and sustainable practices.
  • Strengthen parental involvement in every stage of a child’s growth.
